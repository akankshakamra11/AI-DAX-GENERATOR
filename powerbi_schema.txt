POWER BI MODEL: E-commerce Sales

TABLE: Orders
Columns:
- OrderDate
- CustomerID
- ProductID
- Quantity
- UnitPrice
- OrderID
- Discount
- ShippingCost
- Region
- PaymentMethod

TABLE: Returns
Columns:
- Return_ID
- Order_ID
- Return_Date
- Return_Reason

TABLE: Products
Columns:
- productid
- ProductName
- Category
- SubCategory
- UnitCost
- UnitPrice

TABLE: Customers
Columns:
- Customer_ID
- First_Name
- Last_Name
- Email
- City
- Country

TABLE: Region Info
Columns:
- Region_Code
- Region_Name

RECOMMENDED RELATIONSHIPS:
- Customers[Customer_ID] one-to-many Orders[CustomerID]
- Products[productid] one-to-many Orders[ProductID]
- Region Info[Region_Code] one-to-many Orders[Region]
- Orders[OrderID] one-to-many Returns[Order_ID]

DATA-TYPE GUIDANCE:
- Orders[OrderDate] is a date.
- Returns[Return_Date] is a date.
- Orders[Quantity] is a whole number.
- Orders[UnitPrice] is a decimal/currency value.
- Orders[Discount] is a percentage stored as a whole number, such as 10 for 10%.
- Orders[ShippingCost] is a decimal/currency value.
- Products[UnitCost] and Products[UnitPrice] are decimal/currency values.
- Orders[OrderID] and Returns[Order_ID] are matching order identifiers.

BUSINESS RULES:
- Gross Sales = Quantity × UnitPrice.
- Discount Amount = Gross Sales × Discount ÷ 100.
- Net Product Sales = Gross Sales − Discount Amount.
- Order Revenue including shipping = Net Product Sales + ShippingCost.
- Product Cost = Quantity × related Products[UnitCost].
- Gross Profit = Net Product Sales − Product Cost.
- A returned order is an order whose Orders[OrderID] exists in Returns[Order_ID].
- Distinct customers should be counted using Customers[Customer_ID] or Orders[CustomerID], depending on the visual context.
- Distinct orders should be counted using Orders[OrderID].
- Region analysis should use Region Info[Region_Name] when the relationship is active.
- Product analysis should use Products[Category], Products[SubCategory], and Products[ProductName].
- Customer geography analysis should use Customers[Country] and Customers[City].
